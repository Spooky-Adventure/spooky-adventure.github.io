Introduction

Spooky Adventure is a ghostly hack of New Super Mario Bros. Wii, currently in development. The game takes place on a haunted island with deep caverns below the surface. Traverse through several spooky levels on your quest to save Princess Peach once more!

The mod can be legally played on any Wii console, as well as in Dolphin emulator. Once the mod releases, this site will host several guides for installing and running Spooky Adventure. Until then, stay tuned!
